Founded in 2016, CSS - Clintax Software Solution Academy was established to redefine technical education in Chennai.

CSS Academy was built as a "Forge" where students are treated as junior associates. Our unique "Audit-Based Learning" methodology for Tally and GST has become the benchmark for financial training in North Chennai.
